A space is $T_2$ (or Hausdorff) if, given any two distinct points $a$ and $b$, there are disjoint open sets $O_a$ and $O_b$ containing $a$ and $b$, respectively.

Defined on page 11 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4612-6290-9}}.
